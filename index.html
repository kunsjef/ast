How It Works
Tick base unit
2.4 sec, 36,000/day For short delays, microwave impatience, and waiting before saying “so anyway...”
Slice 50 Ticks
2 min, 720/day Slightly longer than a normal minute in spirit, but now mathematically accountable.
Round 12 Slices
24 min, 60/day The standard unit for meetings, sports breaks, and optimistic maintenance windows.
Block 6 Rounds
2.4 hr, 10/day The main planning unit. Ten per day, five per Shift, suspiciously tidy.
Shift 5 Blocks
12 hr, 2/day First Shift is midnight to noon. Second Shift is noon to midnight.
