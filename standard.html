Calendar Rules date.system

Each year contains 13 regular months of 28 days, totaling 364 scheduled days.

The weekly roster follows modern local weekdays: Monday is Manday, Sunday is Liberty Day.

Adjustment Rules exception.system

National Adjustment Day occurs after the 364 regular days of a normal year.

Leap years add Bonus Adjustment Day. Both days are outside month and week jurisdiction.
